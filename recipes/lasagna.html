World's most delicious lasagna at home!

Each bite unveils a harmonious blend of melted cheeses, tangy tomato sauce, and hearty fillings, promising a gratifying experience for the senses. Crafting the perfect lasagna is an art form, and this recipe promises to guide you through each step with ease. Begin by layering al dente pasta sheets with a sumptuous mixture of seasoned ground beef, sweet Italian sausage, and finely diced onions and garlic, sautéed to aromatic perfection. Next, generously spread a velvety ricotta and spinach mixture, adding a touch of vibrancy and depth to every layer. With each slice, savor the melding of flavors and textures, as the essence of homemade comfort fills the air, inviting you to savor every moment of this timeless culinary delight.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound (450g) ground beef
  • 1/2 pound (225g) sweet Italian sausage, casings removed
  • 1 onion, finely diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (24-ounce) jar of marinara sauce
  • 1 (15-ounce) container ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup chopped spinach (fresh or frozen)
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Step by step to prepare delicious lasagna

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until softened and fragrant.
  4. Add the ground beef and Italian sausage to the skillet, breaking it up with a spoon, and cook until browned and cooked through. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Stir in the marinara sauce and simmer for about 10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  6. In a separate bowl, mix together the ricotta cheese and chopped spinach until well combined.
  7. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  8. Arrange a layer of cooked lasagna noodles over the sauce.
  9. Spread half of the ricotta-spinach mixture evenly over the noodles.
  10. Sprinkle with a layer of shredded mozzarella cheese.
  11. Repeat the layers with the remaining ingredients, finishing with a layer of meat sauce and a generous sprinkle of Parmesan cheese on top.
  12. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
  13. Remove the foil and continue baking for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly and the lasagna is heated through.
  14. Let the lasagna cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy!
